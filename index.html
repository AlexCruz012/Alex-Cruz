Who are we?

Oasis is a nonprofit organisation set to combat
food waste with help from local supermarkets and distributors.

Our "pay as you feel" policy (where you can pay any amount for
your meal) makes it so it is accessible for anyone.

We collect all FRESH FOOD that would otherwise go to landfill and create a changeable daily menu.

Our food is NOT rotten, unusable or expired, we collect fresh nutritional food only!

You can also subscribe to our "pay as you feel" fresh fruit and vegs cupboard goods box, collected from our warehouse attached to our restaurant.
